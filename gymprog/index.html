Benchpress

The bench press is a compound exercise that targets the muscles of the upper body. It involves lying on a bench and pressing weight upward using either a barbell or a pair of dumbbells.

Squats

An exercise in which a standing person lowers to a position in which the torso is erect and the knees are deeply bent and then rises to an upright position.

Legpress

The leg press is a compound weight training exercise in which the individual pushes a weight or resistance away from them using their legs..
